# Pricing Page Copy V1

## Goal

This document provides the first usable pricing page copy for Yansilu.

It is written to support:

- visual design
- landing page implementation
- pricing and conversion review

## Pricing Strategy

Yansilu should not sell “more AI output” as the primary value.

It should sell:

- a more complete thinking workflow
- stronger knowledge-to-wisdom support
- a clearer path from notes to writing
- a more durable long-term research and writing practice

## Page Structure

1. Hero
2. Billing toggle
3. Plan cards
4. Why upgrade
5. Feature comparison
6. FAQ
7. Final CTA

## Section 6: FAQ

### FAQ 1

Question:

`研思录和普通笔记软件有什么区别？`

Answer:

`普通笔记软件更擅长保存与整理内容。研思录更强调把资料一步步压缩成观点、索引结构与写作路径。`

### FAQ 2

Question:

`Free 可以长期使用吗？`

Answer:

`可以。Free 适合建立基础工作流，并持续进行本地优先的资料整理与笔记积累。`

### FAQ 3

Question:

`Pro 主要提升在哪里？`

Answer:

`Pro 主要提升在知识到智慧辅助、结构建议、写作脚手架和更强的检索支持，适合更高频的研究与写作场景。`

### FAQ 4

Question:

`付费后内容是否仍然属于我？`

Answer:

`是。研思录的核心方向仍然是本地优先、可迁移与可导出。`

### FAQ 5

Question:

`AI 会不会替我自动写完整内容？`

Answer:

`不会。研思录中的 AI 角色是辅助提炼、组织和检查，而不是绕过你的理解直接生成最终思想。`

### FAQ 6

Question:

`可以取消订阅吗？`

Answer:

`可以。Pro 订阅应支持按账单周期管理与取消。`

## Notes for Design

The pricing page should feel:

- calm
- confident
- serious
- non-gimmicky

Avoid:

- aggressive scarcity language
- inflated AI claims
- loud “best deal” SaaS patterns
- fake productivity urgency

Recommended emphasis:

- long-term practice
- authorship
- clarity
- structure
- continuity from notes to writing
